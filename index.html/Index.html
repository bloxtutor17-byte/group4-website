BloxTutor – Roblox Tutorial Stream
Objective Audience Key Message Strategies Timeline Budget Evaluation Media

1. Objective / Goal

We want to achieve a platform where players can learn and improve their Roblox skills through fun and engaging tutorials, while building an active and supportive community on Twitch and TikTok.

2. Target Audience

Our target audience includes Roblox players of all ages, especially beginners and intermediate gamers who want to improve their gameplay, learn scripting, or explore Roblox Studio. We also aim to reach aspiring game creators and viewers who enjoy tips, strategies, and inspiration.

3. Key Message

Learning Roblox can be fun and easy with BloxTutor. We want viewers to feel inspired, confident, and motivated to create and explore while being part of a supportive community.

4. Promotional Strategies

We will promote our content mainly on TikTok and Twitch. On TikTok, we’ll upload stream clips, tips, and funny moments using hashtags like #BloxTutor and #RobloxTutorial. On Twitch, we will stay active by streaming regularly and interacting with viewers to encourage growth and engagement.

5. Timeline

Week 1: Prepare content and set up Twitch/TikTok accounts.
Week 2: Begin posting short clips on TikTok.
Week 3: Launch first Twitch livestream.
Weekly: Continue streaming and posting tutorials.

6. Budget

Total budget: ₱1,000–₱1,500 for microphone, lighting, and possible internet upgrade. Other tasks like editing and promoting will be done using free tools.

7. Evaluation / Measurement

Success will be measured through followers, views, comments, watch time, and feedback on TikTok and Twitch. More interaction, questions, and positive comments indicate that the tutorials are effective and helpful.
